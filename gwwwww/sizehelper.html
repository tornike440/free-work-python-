Size Helper

    Customer Size Guide

  1. Usual Size (Regular Fit):For a standard, comfortable fit, we recommend choosing your usual size. Our products are designed to be true to size, and you can expect them to fit well and comfortably according to your regular size.
  2. Oversized Look If you're aiming for an oversized look or want some extra room for a more relaxed style, we suggest sizing up once. This will give you a looser and more relaxed fit, perfect for achieving that trendy oversized appearance.

    3. Measurement Tips:To ensure you select the right size, we also recommend taking your measurements and referring to our detailed size chart down below. This will help you match your measurements with our product dimensions, providing additional guidance to guarantee the perfect fit.

    Need Further Assistance?If you're still unsure about the sizing, our customer support team is here to assist you. Feel free to reach out to us with any questions or concerns about choosing the right size. We want you to be happy with your purchase and look great in our products!
